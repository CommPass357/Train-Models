# v1.0.0

Initial complete digital manufacturing package for the HO Union Pacific #80 coal turbine.

## Added
- Three unit shells: A/control, center turbine, and coal tender.
- Three independent chassis: powered-ready A/control, powered-ready center turbine, rolling tender.
- Resin STL, FDM STL, 3MF, faceted STEP, DXF, and SVG manufacturing outputs.
- BOM, build guide, paint/decal plan, research notes, manifest, and validation report.

## Known Limits
- STEP files are faceted interchange exports generated without a local CAD kernel.
- Donor drive mounting should be adjusted from measured parts before final metal cutting.
